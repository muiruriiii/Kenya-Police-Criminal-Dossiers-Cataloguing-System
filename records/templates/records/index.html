Digitized Police Station

This system aims at improving the police system in Kenya by digitizing the system. From the system a citizen is able to report a CrimesDisplay online and also track the status of their case. The citizen can also upload their evidence , statements as well as any other necessary information into the system. A case is assigned to an officer who is able to key in the details of a case into the system which will be stored in the database. The case is only cleared after approval by the OCS.

OUR SERVICES
Report Crime

Now you have the priviledge to report a crime online instead of having to go to the police station to report the crime.In this application, you have the ability to report a crime anonymously as well. You are required to provide as much information as you can, regarding the incident including images or any audios and videos.

Download Forms

This portal enables the complainant to get the required form based on their case. There are 3 types of forms;abstract,accident abstract and p3.

Click Here to Get A Form
Statements and Evidence

This portal enables the complainants and witnesses to record their statements and upload evidence. They have the ability to upload any files that can be of help in the investigation of the case.

Click Here to Record A Statement
Track Case

This portal gives the complainant an opportunity to track the progress of one's case after having it reported. They are able to view the details of their case;the officer that was assigned to their case, the evidence files as well as their OB number.
