P3

A P3 form is used to request for medical examination by a Medical Officer of Health, in order to determine the nature and extent of the bodily injury sustained by a complainant(s) in assault cases. The P3 form is filled out by the police officer assigned to the case indicating details of the complainant or suspects. The form should also be filled by a registered medical officer after a full body checkup has been carried out.
